Why Remodel

A Kitchen That Works as Hard as You Do

Whether you're looking to modernize an outdated layout, maximize storage, or completely transform the look and feel of your kitchen, Honest Renovations delivers. With over 50 years of combined experience and in-house expertise in plumbing, electrical, cabinetry, and tiling, we handle every aspect of your kitchen remodel — all under one roof, with full transparency at every step.

What's Included

Everything Your Kitchen Needs

Custom Cabinets & Millwork

Built-to-measure cabinetry and custom millwork designed to maximize your storage and suit your style.

Countertops & Surfaces

Quartz, granite, butcher block, or laminate — we source and install the right surface for your needs and budget.

Plumbing & Fixtures

Sink relocation, new plumbing lines, fixture upgrades — all handled by our experienced in-house plumbers.

Electrical & Lighting

Pot lights, under-cabinet lighting, new circuits for appliances — our electricians bring your kitchen to life.

Backsplash & Tiling

Custom tile backsplashes, floor tile, and stonework installed with precision and attention to detail.

Hardwood, luxury vinyl, tile, or engineered wood — we install the flooring that ties your kitchen together.

Painting & Finishing

Appliance Installation

We coordinate and install all appliances, including range hoods, dishwashers, and built-in refrigerators.

How It Works

Our Renovation Process

We keep you informed and in control at every stage — from the first design conversation to the final detail.

Free Consultation & Design

We visit your home, discuss your vision, take measurements, and provide a detailed, transparent quote with no hidden costs.

Demolition & Prep

We carefully remove existing fixtures, cabinetry, and surfaces while protecting the rest of your home throughout.

Construction & Installation

Our tradespeople handle plumbing, electrical, framing, cabinetry, countertops, tiling, and flooring with skilled precision.

Finishing & Walkthrough

We complete all painting, trim, and final touches, then walk you through the finished space to ensure your satisfaction.
