Who We Are

About Honest Renovations

Built on a simple idea — that homeowners deserve quality craftsmanship, straight answers, and a team they can trust from the first handshake to the final nail.

Our Story

Renovations Built on Integrity

Honest Renovations was founded on a belief that the renovation industry needed something it too often lacks: honesty. Co-founders Jaswant Dhillon and Kulwant Sandhu combined their decades of hands-on experience to build a company where every client is treated with respect, every budget is taken seriously, and every project is completed with the same pride they would put into their own homes.

With over 50 years of combined experience between them, Jaswant and Kulwant bring deep expertise across the full range of residential renovation trades — plumbing, electrical, drywall, tiling, painting, and custom cabinetry and millwork. Their trusted team extends that expertise to painting, flooring, landscaping, gas fitting, appliance installation, and millwork, giving clients a single point of contact for every aspect of their renovation.

Serving Metro Vancouver and the Lower Mainland, Honest Renovations is a company built on three unchanging commitments: punctuality, budget adherence, and integrity. When you work with us, you'll always know where your project stands, what it costs, and when it will be done.

What We Know Best

Areas of Expertise

Our co-founders bring direct, hands-on mastery across every core trade that goes into a quality home renovation.

Plumbing

From rough-in to finish plumbing, our founders have deep expertise in residential plumbing systems — supply lines, drain work, fixture installation, and everything in between. Whether it's a kitchen sink relocation, a new basement bathroom, or a complete replumb, the work is done correctly, efficiently, and to code. You won't face leaks, callbacks, or inspections failed.

Electrical

Our electrical expertise covers panel work, circuit additions, pot light installation, under-cabinet and outdoor lighting, and full electrical rough-ins for new spaces. Safety and compliance are non-negotiable — every electrical job we do is performed to BC Electrical Code. Homeowners can expect clean, professional wiring and full transparency about what was done and why.

Drywall

Hanging, taping, mudding, and finishing — drywall is one of the most skill-dependent trades in renovation work, and it shows in the final result. Our founders bring years of experience producing smooth, flawless walls and ceilings that paint beautifully and stand up over time. No ridges, no visible seams, no texture inconsistencies — just a clean, professional surface.

Tiling

Precision tiling transforms a bathroom or kitchen from ordinary to exceptional. Our team handles all tile formats — large-format floor tile, subway tile, natural stone, mosaic accents, and custom shower surrounds. Every tile is set level, grouted cleanly, and sealed correctly for long-term durability. We take the time to plan layouts that look intentional and professionally designed.

Painting

A great paint job is about far more than colour selection. Proper surface preparation, clean cut lines, consistent coverage, and the right products for each surface make the difference between work that looks good now and work that holds up for years. Our team handles interior and exterior painting with the same care and attention to prep that defines everything we do.

Cabinet & Millwork

Custom cabinetry and millwork are where a renovation truly becomes personal. Our co-founders bring expert-level skill in designing, building, and installing cabinets, shelving, built-ins, and trim work that fit a space perfectly and are built to last. From kitchen cabinetry to basement bar built-ins to custom closet systems, the craftsmanship in every piece reflects a deep pride in the work.
