Our Services

Bathroom Remodelling

Transform your bathroom into a personal retreat. From precise tile work to custom vanities and plumbing upgrades, we build bathrooms that balance luxury with lasting function.

Request a Free Quote
Why Remodel

A Space You'll Look Forward to Every Day

A well-renovated bathroom is one of the highest-return investments in your home — and one of the most satisfying. At Honest Renovations, we bring expertise in tiling, plumbing, electrical, and millwork together to deliver bathrooms that look stunning and work flawlessly. Whether you want a sleek, modern spa feel or a warm, classic design, we'll bring your vision to life with craftsmanship that shows in every detail.

What's Included

Everything Your Bathroom Deserves

Tile Work

Floor tile, shower tile, feature walls, and niches — installed with expert precision for a flawless, lasting finish.

Plumbing & Fixtures

Shower systems, soaker tubs, toilets, and all plumbing rough-in and finish work handled by our in-house plumbers.

Vanities & Storage

Custom or semi-custom vanities with countertop, integrated storage solutions, and mirror or medicine cabinet installation.

Electrical & Lighting

Vanity lighting, pot lights, exhaust fans, and heated flooring electrical — all installed safely and to code.

Glass & Shower Enclosures

Frameless glass shower doors and enclosures that give your bathroom a clean, open, high-end appearance.

Flooring

Heated tile floors, luxury vinyl, or natural stone — we install flooring that's both beautiful and moisture-safe.

Painting & Trim

Moisture-resistant paint, baseboards, and all finish trim work to complete the look with a polished, seamless result.

In-Floor Heating (Optional)

Hydronic or electric in-floor heating systems for added comfort — a favourite upgrade in BC's cooler months.

How It Works

Our Renovation Process

We make your bathroom renovation as smooth and stress-free as possible, with clear communication from start to finish.

Consultation & Design

We discuss your needs, take measurements, help you choose materials and finishes, and provide a detailed, honest quote.

Demolition & Waterproofing

Careful removal of existing fixtures and finishes, followed by proper waterproofing preparation — the most critical step.

Plumbing, Electrical & Tile

All rough-in work is completed first, then tiling, fixtures, vanity, and glass are installed with precision and care.

Finishing & Walkthrough

Painting, trim, accessories, and final cleanup — followed by a full walkthrough to confirm you're completely satisfied.
