Outdoor Living

Your Backyard, Transformed

Metro Vancouver's mild climate makes outdoor living possible for much of the year — and a beautifully built patio or outdoor kitchen is one of the best ways to enjoy it. Honest Renovations handles everything from poured concrete and paving to full outdoor kitchen builds complete with gas fitting, sinks, and countertops. Our team also handles landscaping, lighting, and privacy structures to create an outdoor space that's truly an extension of your home.

What's Included

Everything You Need for Outdoor Living

Patio Construction

Concrete, natural stone, pavers, or tile — we design and build patios that are level, durable, and beautiful for decades.

Outdoor Kitchen Build

Custom outdoor kitchen structures with built-in BBQ, countertops, storage, and prep space — designed for real outdoor cooking.

Gas Line & Fitting

Licensed gas fitting for BBQ connections, outdoor fireplaces, and fire pits — installed safely and to code.

Outdoor Plumbing

Outdoor sink connections and drainage for a fully functional outdoor kitchen — no trips inside for water.

Landscaping

Garden beds, grading, sod, plants, and retaining walls — our team shapes and finishes the landscape around your new outdoor space.

Outdoor Lighting

String lights, pot lights, pathway lighting, and security lighting to make your outdoor space usable and inviting after dark.

Pergolas & Shade Structures

Custom pergolas, awnings, and shade sails to keep your outdoor space comfortable through sun and light rain.

Fencing & Privacy Screens

Cedar fencing, lattice screens, and privacy panels that define your space and give you the seclusion you want.

How It Works

Our Build Process

Outdoor projects require careful coordination across trades. We manage the entire build so you don't have to.

Design & Quote

We walk your outdoor space, discuss your vision and priorities, and provide a clear, itemized quote with no guesswork.

Site Preparation

Grading, excavation, and any underground utilities (gas, plumbing) are laid out and roughed in before surface work begins.

Construction

Patio surface, outdoor kitchen structure, gas and water connections, pergola or shade structure — built to last.

Landscaping & Finishing

Lighting, planting, fencing, and final cleanup. We leave your outdoor space ready to enjoy from day one.
